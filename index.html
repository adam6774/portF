About Me

Hi there! I'm Adam, a passionate Computer Science student with a deep-rooted fascination for the world of cybersecurity and ethical hacking. My journey into this field began with curiosity about how systems work and how they can be secured.

I thrive on solving complex technical challenges, which is why I'm an avid participant in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. These challenges sharpen my problem-solving skills and expose me to various security domains, from web exploitation to reverse engineering.

My goal is to leverage my growing expertise to contribute to a safer digital world. I'm constantly exploring new tools, methodologies, and vulnerabilities, driven by a desire to understand and mitigate cyber threats. I'm eager to apply my skills in real-world scenarios and grow as a penetration testing professional.

Key Interests:
